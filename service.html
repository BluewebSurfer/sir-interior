WALLPAPER:

Wallpapers are the best option when you want to transform the space drastically without much effort and in a cost efficient way. Wallpapers with regular repeating patterns or a large single design spread over a set of sheets, whatever is your choice, we make it done for you.

STRUCTURE PAINT:

Textured paint contains gypsum, sand, metal, ribbon, lace etc which is used for creating a rough, uneven texture on the wall. Our team uses various tools to achieve different looks with these texture paints. It is considered as an alternative to wall papers and stimulates your sense of sight and touch.

WOODEN FLOOR:

The floor in our opinion must look as beautiful as it should be strong and durable to take the pressures and weights of everyday life. We provide you with wooden floors whose beauty lasts forever. Parquet floors with the wood of your choice, are available in many shapes and colours with beautiful surface effects.

MARBLE:

Marbles are strong, durable, and sturdy It revamps your house without looking out of place as it merges with any background. Marbles are an excellent option for both walls and floors.It adds a dash of style and gives a subtle modern touch to the space. It gives your bedroom a look of luxury.

3D FLOOR:

3D flooring gives a whole new meaning to the concept of flooring. It is a photo or a painting which extends a 3D effect to your floor. It has stunning visual effects.It is resistant to acids, chlorine, bleaches and daily cleaners.It is immune to dirt, dust and water and is fire resistant.Choosing the correct pattern and colour can make your space look more spacious and beautiful.3D flooring pictures have a high resolution- at least 300dpi.

FALSE CEILING:

The materials that we work with for false ceilings are gypsum, plaster of paris, wood. We are expert in making single layered false ceiling, multi layered false ceiling, pop false ceiling,and coffered false ceiling, We are professionals in this field with expertise in false ceilings of different patterns, technologies, and illusions.

ELECTRIC AND PLUMBING:

Procuring all electric and plumbing materials and fixing is done by us. We make sure the finishing is perfect , as any work done by professionals should look.
